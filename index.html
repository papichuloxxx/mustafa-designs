About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Construction & Engineering

Mustafa Designs is a multi-disciplinary construction and engineering consultancy firm based in Zimbabwe. Under the leadership of Managing Director S. Mustafa, we deliver comprehensive services spanning architectural design, quantity surveying, project management, and engineering solutions.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction has established us as a reliable partner for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. We combine technical expertise with creative vision to bring your construction dreams to life.
